About the University

Aiming to bring about change and progress through education, the Thai Kannammal Educational Trust was constituted in 1985 and started Thai Moogambigai Polytechnic College. Within three years of its inception, The M.G.R. Engineering College was founded in 1988, and the Thai Moogambigai Dental College was started in the year 1991. Both the colleges acquired Deemed University status in 2003 as Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute as per the orders of the University Grants Commission, New Delhi and the Union Ministry of Human Resources and Development, Government of India, New Delhi. Our programs offered under different faculties are affiliated with various governmental organization and approved by the Special Autonomous Status Provided by Govt. of India, NAAC A+, ISO 9001-2015 Certified, IAO accreditation.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering aims to impart high quality education to under graduate and post graduate students as well as to carry out cutting edge technology in research on various disciplines of Computer Science and Engineering. All the programmes offered by the Department focus on preparing students for a wide range of Software domain, equipping and moulding future technocrats with high caliber at all levels to meet the expectations of stakeholders. The department is accredited by NBA and ABET.

About the Conference

NCICT'25 is a two days National Conference organized by the Department of CSE through hybrid mode. In today's digital world where everything is connected, the role of Computing Science has changed dramatically over the last couple of decades. The 10th National Conference on Innovative Computing Techniques under the theme of MATRIX mainly focuses on research topics in IoT, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum Computing, Edge Computing, Cloud Computing, Cryptocurrency, Cyber Security, Clean Technology and the topics of interest are not limited. The objective of the conference is to exchange innovative ideas among Academicians, Researchers and Industry experts to enhance secured digital transformation.
